What is
ICICyTA 2021?

The 1st International Virtual Conference in Intelligent Cybernetics Technology & Applications 2021 (ICyTA 2021) with a theme “Cybernetic Technology for Sustainable Well-being” will be held on December 1-2, 2021. This virtual conference is organized by Human Centric Engineering Telkom University (Tel-U) and Pusat Penelitian Teknologi Informasi dan Komunikasi (PPTIK) Institut Teknologi Bandung (ITB) Indonesia, with co-organization by Institute of Autonomous System (IAS) / Institute of Health and Analytics (IHA), Universiti Teknologi PETRONAS (UTP), Malaysia. ICyTA 2021 invites researchers to share their knowledge, especially in the field of cybernetics which also includes computational intelligence, Internet of Things (IoT), biomedical engineering, and applications. The conference will be sponsored by IEEE Indonesian Section
